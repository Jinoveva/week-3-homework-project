Weather app

A weather app is a mobile application that provides accurate and real-time weather forecasts, alerts, and weather-related information. The app typically features an interactive map that shows current weather conditions, such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and cloud cover. Users can set location-based alerts for severe weather events, such as thunderstorms or snowstorms, to keep them informed about potential hazards. Additional features may include daily and hourly weather forecasts, pollen levels, UV index, and air quality information. The app is usually designed with a user-friendly interface and is available for iOS and Android devices.
